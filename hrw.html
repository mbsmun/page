TOPIC A: Examining Hollywood’s colorism and how it has normalized racial inequality in media representation.

TOPIC B: Analyzing the global impact of modern slavery and its repercussions.

Inclusion within Hollywood cinema has made great advances in recent decades. Colorism is already an almost null and not very used term within people during these days…right? On the other hand, people currently believe that slavery was completely abolished centuries ago; nonetheless, this is further from the truth. Thousands of people are victims all around the world of modern slavery. Welcome to our committee for this year’s MBS MUN, HRW (Human Rights Watch). This year we will be debating upon these two significant themes; 1) Hollywood’s colorism and how it has normalized racial inequality in media representation; 2) The global impact of modern slavery and its repercussions. The delegates participating in the HRW Committee must discuss thoroughly, work together, develop effective strategies and propose solutions to address these issues and make the world a better place.
